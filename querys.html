Company responsible

Query to display, given a company, the company data, responsible professor, and company contact personnel.

Intership hosts

Query to display, given a educational cycle and a school year, the companies that have hosted internships and the number of internships hosted.

Group tutor & FCT placements

Query that displays, given a school group and a school year, the details of the group's tutor teacher and the companies where they are carrying out their FCTs, along with the number of placements hosted.

Rnternship details

Query that displays, given a company and a school year, the FCT details.
